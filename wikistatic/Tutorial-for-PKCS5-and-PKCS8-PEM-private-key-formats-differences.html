  • PKCS#8 plain private key
  • PKCS#5 encrypted private key
  • PKCS#8 encrypted private key

To convert from PKCS#5 private key to encrypted PKCS#8 private key:

% openssl pkcs8 -in p5plain.pem -topk8 -v2 des3 -out p8enc.pem -passout pass:passwd
